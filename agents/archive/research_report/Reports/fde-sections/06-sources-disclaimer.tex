\subsection{Limitations}

\begin{itemize}
    \item FDE is an emerging, non-standardized role. Job title usage
        varies significantly across companies, making cross-company
        comparisons approximate.
    \item Compensation data is drawn from job postings and self-reported
        sources; actual packages may differ.
    \item Palantir data is disproportionately represented due to
        the company's pioneering role. Newer entrants have less
        publicly available data.
    \item Some practitioner quotes are drawn from anonymous Glassdoor
        reviews and may not represent official company positions.
    \item The +1,165\% growth figure covers Jan--Oct 2025 vs.\ the
        same period in 2024. Growth off a low base can overstate
        absolute market size.
\end{itemize}
